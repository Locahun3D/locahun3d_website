CHAPTER 01 — WHAT IS 3DGS —
CH. 01 What is 3D Gaussian Splatting?

A new 3D representation that brings
a space back exactly as it is.

3D Gaussian Splatting (3DGS) is a technique that
densely reconstructs real space from multi-viewpoint photos and video.

01 / PHOTOREAL

Optical Photorealism

Translucent objects, reflections, and subtle lighting changes
that were hard to capture with conventional mesh + texture
are preserved just as they looked at the time of capture.

02 / REALTIME

Real-Time Rendering

A high-speed rendering method close to rasterization lets you
move the viewpoint without the heavy inference of NeRF.
It ties directly to the speed of decision-making.

03 / PORTABLE

Lightweight Delivery

A few tens to a few hundred MB per scene.
Because it runs on WebGL / WebGPU,
you can build a delivery format that anyone can open on just an iPhone, Mac, iPad, or Laptop.

02 / SCAN — CAPTURE METHOD

SLAM Scanning with the XGRIDS PortalCam

The XGRIDS PortalCam is a SLAM-type handheld / wearable scanner
that integrates LiDAR with multiple cameras.
Just walking through a space outputs it in Gaussian Splatting format.

  • Output.ply (Gaussian Splatting) / .lcc / .ply (point cloud) / .Rad
  • MethodLiDAR + Multi-cam SLAM (real-time localization)
  • RangeContinuous scanning from a single interior room to block scale (outdoor 100m+)
  • Accuracycm-order point-cloud accuracy. Practical enough as a baseline for distance measurement
  • SpeedRoughly 5–10 min per room / 15–30 min for an outdoor location
  • Output Size50–500 MB / scene (small interior to mid-size block)
+ DRONE CAPTURE

For wide-area locations or spots you can't reach on foot — rooftops, waterfronts, level changes —
we also support 3DGS reconstruction from drone aerial capture (multi-viewpoint shooting).
Combined with the PortalCam's ground scan,
you bring back a seamless 3D space spanning sky ↔ ground in one pass.

CHAPTER 03 — BEYOND SCOUT —
CH. 03 Applied Workflows — Beyond Location Scouting

Captured 3DGS carries straight through to compositing, previz, and lighting
— reusable as-is.

3DGS data isn't limited to location scouting.
Bring it into tools like Blender / Unreal / Unity / Maya / After Effects
and you can span 3D tracking baselines, animation previz, lighting checks,
and downstream VFX compositing — all from a single set of real-space data.

01 / TRACKING

3D Tracking Baseline

Because 3DGS is captured at real-world scale, it can be used directly as a geometry baseline for camera tracking and 3D compositing in After Effects / Mocha / SynthEyes / Blender. Alignment accuracy with live-action footage improves dramatically.

02 / PREVIZ

Animation Previz

Import into Unreal Engine 5 / Blender / Maya and pre-check CG characters and camera moves overlaid on real space. Because you can nail down blocking, runtime, and angles before the shoot, the speed of on-the-day decisions is on another level.

03 / LIGHTING

Lighting & Visualization

Because 3DGS is specialized for lightweight, real-time rendering, you can swap environment light or a pseudo-HDRI and instantly iterate on time of day, weather, and light placement. It also works well as a simulation environment for the art and lighting departments.

CHAPTER 04 — CHROME APP —
CH. 04 The "Locahun 3D" Chrome Application

No installation required.
Open it in your browser and start right away.

CHROME APPLICATION

"Locahun 3D" is a 3DGS viewer and virtual-camera application
that runs inside the Chrome browser.
Just drag and drop a scanned .ply / .Rad file, and
real-device-grade lens simulation, distance measurement, environment lighting,
and metadata-tagged JPEG export are all completed inside the browser.

⚡

No Installation Required

Launches instantly with just Chrome. Common to Mac / Windows / Linux. Roll out the same environment to your whole crew at once.

🛡

Local Processing

3DGS data stays entirely within the browser. No uploads to a server — safe even for confidential locations.

💻

Runs on Any Device

Windows / Mac / iPad / iPhone — check on the same viewer from any device. Crew can review instantly on whatever gear is at hand.

📡

Works Offline

After the first load, save the PF and it runs with no internet connection. Works on location, in transit, or for indoor shoots with no signal.

Scan Conditions

  • On rainy days, scanning may not be possible for equipment reasons, so the date and time may be changed.
  • For drone surveys, a preparation period may be required due to flight-permit arrangements.
  • If shooting dates clash, we may need to adjust the date and time.
  • For locations far from the Kanto region, we may charge separate travel expenses depending on the situation.
  • If there is a better approach than 3DGS, we will suggest it. On request, we can connect you with partner companies.

If you have a vision you'd like to realize beyond 3DGS representation,
